Guitarist, Pianist & Composer

Biography

Sidharth Sarangi is a classical guitarist, pianist and composer from Odisha, India. He has won prizes at several national and international competitions, including first prize at the Global Guitar Gita International Guitar Festival and Competition. In 2022, he became the first person from India to be accepted at the Royal College of Music, London. His repertoire ranges from Bach suites to modern composers like Gary Ryan, Leo Brouwer, Pavel Steidl, and Frederic Rzewski. He has performed extensively across India and internationally, and his music has been featured on NPR Music and The Great American Folk Show.
